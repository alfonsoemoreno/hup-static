About the hotel

The Last Paradise Hotel is a charming hotel or "green refuge".

Built in 1997. The construction has been completely made with native woods (cipres, maÃ±io, lenga). We, the owners, are a Spanish couple who fell in love with Cochrane and these very last virgin lands left on our planet. That is the reason for our hotel name Last Paradise. It is probably the last natural, pristine and virgin paradise of the world.

Landscape Around Cochrane

In between the highest mountains of Patagonia you will find The Cerro San Lorenzo close to 4,000 mts (12,000 feet) and The San Valentin Mountain, over 4,000 mts (13,000 feet), which is the highest mountain in Patagonia and The Northern Icefield. Being the biggest river of Chile, The Baker River, receives the water of the second biggest lake of South America, General Carrera Lake. Yo can drink the water of the second largest potable water reserves on earth, which are The Northern and Southern Icefields, which are over 200,000 square kilometers wide and 3 kms deep of pure ice. Patagonia is the last refuge of the patagonic deer (huemul), where they are able to live pure and free. The different areas of the region, even though simple with title, are indescribabley beautiful and why this region is The Last Paradise.
